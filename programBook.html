Specifications

The program book will be printed in black and white. It will be formatted at 8.5"x11", with a half-inch margin on all sides. Please ensure that your ad conforms to the size specifications listed above. Electronic submission at dilloprogrambook@armadillocon.org is preferred. We accept PDF, TIFF, JPG, and PNG formats. Image resolution should be 300 pixels/inch or higher, to assure that your ad will look good when printed. Deadline for ad copy is to be determined.
